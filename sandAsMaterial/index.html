ABOUT US



Wernecular is an architecture and design studio that begins from the perspective of the “subjectivity”—exploring how contemporary expression can meaningfully merge with local culture. Our name, a blend of “W” (our founders) and “vernacular,” reflects our mission to redefine contextual design through personal lens.


We are committed to reviving awareness of place, cultural roots, and the authenticity of everyday life. Rather than replicating universal templates, we believe architecture should respond to nature, society, and individual experience—creating spaces that coexist with their environment and resonate with human emotion.
